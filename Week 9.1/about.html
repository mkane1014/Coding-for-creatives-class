About Me

My name is Matthew Kane, native of Peekskill, New York. Graduated from St. Bonaventure University with a B.A. in Strategic Communications & Digital Media in 2015. After graduation I worked as an operations analyst for Prime Clerk LLC. A year later I enrolled in Miami Ad School for their art direction program.

My passions are photography, videography, graphic design, traveling and reading about typography. I strive to become an art director in New York city.
